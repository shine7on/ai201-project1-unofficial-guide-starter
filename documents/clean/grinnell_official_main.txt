Lounges
Main Hall has one lounge located on the first floor and another in its garden level. The first floor lounge has couches, study tables, and a piano. Gardner lounge is in Main's garden level, where Bob's Underground (a student café) and the Free Sound office (a student organization) are also located. 

Kitchens
There are two kitchens available to residents. The main kitchen is located on the fourth floor and is equipped with a stove, oven, microwave, refrigerator, and dining table. The second kitchen is located in the basement and is equipped with a stove, oven, and a refrigerator. 

Storage
Storage space is available in a trunk room located in the building's basement. 

Bathrooms
Six bathrooms are available throughout the hall. There are two gender-inclusive bathrooms on the second, third, and fourth floors. 

Laundry
Residents can use laundry facilities located in Main's basement. Facilities include six washers and dryers. 

Computer Lab
A computer lab with printing facilities is located on the first floor of Cleveland, the residence hall right next to Main.

Recycling
Recycling bins are located on the first floor.

Air Conditioning and Heating
Main Hall offers central heating and air conditioning via portable AC units. The Office of Residence Life works with Facilities Management to review the weather and schedule the installation and removal of portable AC. They are usually removed during fall break and re-installed during spring break.

Elevator
Main has an elevator that can access all floors, but is not ADA-compliant. 

Outdoor Amenities
Outdoor amenities include fire pits and volleyball court, located on Cleve Beach. 

History
Main Hall was named to honor President John Hanson Thomas Main, who conceived of and carried forward the idea of Grinnell as a residential college.
