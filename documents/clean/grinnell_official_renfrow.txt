LOCATION & PURPOSE
Situated at the intersection of Sixth Avenue and Broad Street in downtown Grinnell, Renfrow Hall is a groundbreaking
facility that bridges Grinnell College and the town of Grinnell. Designed to foster collaboration between students
and the broader community, the building features:
• Four stories and 125,000 square feet of space
• Two towers connected by a crossover loggia
LIVING & LEARNING
The hall’s design encourages a unique blend of living and learning, making it a destination for aspiring and
experienced coalition-builders. It also serves as the home base for Grinnell College’s Social Innovator in Residence program, where students engage with leaders driving social change and civic engagement. Renfrow Hall offers:
• Apartment-style living
- student apartments with 110 beds
- Single and double bedrooms within each apartment
- Full kitchens within each apartment
- Shared laundry room on second floor
- Gathering spaces and nooks
SUSTAINABILITY FEATURES
Renfrow Hall embodies Grinnell College’s commitment to
sustainability through:
• Energy Efficiency: High-efficiency glazing, LED lighting,
heat recovery systems, sun shading, and a reflective roof
minimize energy consumption.
• Water Conservation: A 10,000-gallon cistern and lowflow fixtures reduce water usage, while biophilic design
elements such as living walls enhance wellness.
• Transportation: The hall promotes sustainable travel with
indoor bike racks and proximity to the Grinnell College
Shuttle.
• Renewable Energy: Connected to a 4 MW solar farm, the
hall integrates renewable energy into its operations.
• Stormwater Management: A 3,600-square-foot green roof
mitigates stormwater runoff and provides insulation.

CIVIC INNOVATION PAVILION
The Weingart Pavilion within Renfrow Hall is designed to
foster community engagement and features:
• Flexible, light-filled space ideal for gatherings,
workshops, and events
• Seating capacity: 50–60 people; standing capacity: 90
• Features advanced audio-visual technology, including
digital displays, projection capacity, ceiling speakers,
and microphones
• Equipped with a hearing loop for enhanced
accessibility
• Catering kitchenette includes an efficiency sink and
serving space
• Indoor garden serves as a year-round counterpart to
Grinnell’s Central Park, reinforcing the connection to
nature and community
