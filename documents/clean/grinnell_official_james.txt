Lounges
James has three lounges, located on the garden level and on the first and third floors. The first floor lounge has a TV, couches, and a whiteboard. The garden level and third floor lounge have seating.  

Kitchens
The James kitchen is available to residents on the garden level. It is equipped with a stove, oven, microwave, and refrigerator. 

Storage
Storage space is available in a trunk room located on the garden level of James Hall. 

Bathrooms
Eight bathrooms are available throughout James Hall. There is one gender-inclusive bathroom and one gender-specific (women's) bathroom on each floor. 

Laundry
Residents can use laundry facilities on the garden level. Facilities include three washers and six dryers. 

Computer Lab
James Hall residents have easy access to a computer lab located on the first floor of Cleveland Hall. There are six Windows computers, two Macs, and a black-and-white printer. 

Recycling
Recycling bins are located on the first floor. 

Air Conditioning and Heating
James Hall offers central heating and air conditioning via portable AC units. The Office of Residence Life works with Facilities Management to review the weather and schedule the installation and removal of portable AC. They are usually removed during fall break and re-installed during spring break.

Elevators
James Hall does not have elevators.

Outdoor Amenities
Outdoor amenities include fire pits and a volleyball court, located on Cleve Beach. 

History
Mary B. James, for whom James Hall was named, was a respected and revered schoolteacher in the Grinnell community. James Hall is one of five substance-free residence halls on campus.
