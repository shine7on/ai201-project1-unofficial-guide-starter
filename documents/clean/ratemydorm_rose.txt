Part of East Campus so one of the newer and nicer dorms on campus. Generally very quiet compared to other dorms but not much of a community feel like other dorms such as South Campus. Bathrooms are quite nice and there is an elevator as well. One of the few Air conditioned dorms on campus. You will get a lot of sunlight which may be good or bad depending on the person. Would prefer Lazier or Kershaw if since they are slightly closer to the academic buildings but Rose Hall is a bit closer to the Bear (athletics center). Generally a nice calm feel good for the introverted
