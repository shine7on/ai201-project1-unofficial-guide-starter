Lounges
Clark Hall has one beautiful lounge located on the first floor. This lounge is equipped with a large TV, comfortable seating, and several tables to use for studying. It is also conveniently located next to the kitchen. 

Kitchens
Clark Hall has one kitchen on the first floor with a refrigerator, stove with oven, sink, and microwave. This is a great space to cook a meal together. The kitchen is located next to a large lounge.  

Storage
Storage is available in a trunk room located in the garden level of Clark Hall. Please contact C&K Transfer or the Department of Residence Life for inquiries.

Bathrooms
There are three gender-inclusive and one gender-specific bathroom in the garden level of Clark Hall. There are two gender-inclusive bathrooms in the first, second, and third floors. 

Laundry
Residents can use laundry facilities located in the garden level. Clark Hall has three washing machines and six dryers. 

Recycling
Recycling and trash bins are located on each floor of Clark Hall. 

Air Conditioning and Heating
Clark Hall offers both central heating and installable window air conditioning. 

Elevators
Elevators are not available in Clark Hall. All floors are accessible via a central stairwell.

History
Named for Theodore F. Clark, Clark Hall is situated on North Campus.

When President John H.T. Main spoke at the Consecration and Dedication of Clark Hall on June 13, 1920, he noted: "A long time before this group of buildings was realized in brick and mortar, I dreamed about them, and fervently hoped that when they were built that each one would develop in the mind and hearts of those who were to live in it, in some degree, the spiritual ideals that we associate with home life."
