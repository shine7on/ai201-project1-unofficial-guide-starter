Language and Project Houses
Five language houses have existed since 1993. These houses are associated with their academic department. The purpose of the program is to provide students who speak or are learning to speak another language with an environment that is conducive to learning the language, culture, and customs of the country/countries of the language.
A project house consists of students with a common interest and a desire to promote that interest to the campus community. Project houses may change each year.
