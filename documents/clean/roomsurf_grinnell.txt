Grinnell College Dorm Reviews
Main Hall: Awesome if you love partying cause it houses one of the event lounges and it is close to house parties, however there's no air conditioning when school starts and the rooms are fairly small compared to other dorms
Gates Hall: It has been great. Convenient location, but the rooms are a bit small. The community bonds very well. Great floormates and are very active in community events and festivals. Enjoy being there very much.
Loose Hall: Loose Hall is nice if you're looking for community and is usually has a good range of different class years. The quietness really depends on your neighbors since you could have a quiet third-year in a single next door or two party-loving first years.
Norris Hall: Depends on which floor you live on honestly. The all mens floor usually is the most nasty and the all women's floor is in much better condition. Showers usually have at least one thing broken but the bathrooms are extremely nice. Norris used to be the worse dorm on campus but now it's like a hotel (Nicest dorm on campus currently). However, fines are usually frequent as students don't really know how to take care of things. Last year a table was thrown into the 2nd floor window.
